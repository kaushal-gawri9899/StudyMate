  • About StudyMate

  • What is StudyMate?

    Thanks for asking about StudyMate. StudyMate is a platform that gives you an opportunity to connect with your friends, alumni, and teachers, all in one place. StudyMate also offers a variety of Public and Private chat room facilities, that gives room to talk and video call with them.

  • How is it different from other platforms like LinkedIn?

    StudyMate is the only app at the moment that lets you find your mate through a search or among a bunch of other people in a public chat room. You can work together in a team and use features such as screen share, video share and many more.

  • What if I am not a student anymore? How's it still worthy for me?

    It is alright if you’re not a student anymore. You can stay connected with your old mates and keep yourself updated.

  • What if I don't work with an organisation yet? How's it still worthy for me?

    That is alright. You can still find mates from your university or use the public chat room to explore the new opportunities in the job world.

  • Where am I hosted?

    StudyMate is a secure website hosted on powerful AWS Servers. We use SSl to encrypt all the traffic and a virtual private network to safeguard all your private information.

  • How can I hangout with my mates?

    StudyMate offers a variety of Public and Private chat room facilities that give you a room to talk and video call with them.
